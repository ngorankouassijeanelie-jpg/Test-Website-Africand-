Our Approach

Three disciplines.
One mandate. No handoffs.

The Market

Agricultural value chains in Africa attract institutional capital on paper. In practice, transactions stall, not for lack of investor appetite, but because the financial architecture does not exist. Projects are underprepared. Impact metrics go unmeasured. The gap between agricultural enterprises and capital markets remains structural. Africand was built to close that gap.

The Triple Expertise

I

Corporate Finance

We structure the transaction. Due diligence, valuation, deal architecture, financial modelling. Every file is built to the standard that DFIs and institutional investors require, before the first conversation begins.

II

Impact Investment

We connect capital to impact. Blended finance structures, climate finance mechanisms, ESG measurement frameworks. We source and engage investors who deploy at the intersection of financial return and measurable impact.

III

Value Chain Development

We build the underlying capacity. Technical assistance programmes, producer organisation support, market access and quality management. Transactions close faster when the asset is operationally sound.

The Africand Difference

Most advisors cover one discipline.
We cover three, and deploy them
on the same mandate, simultaneously.
